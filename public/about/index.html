Here Be Dragons.

That’s what map makers used to mark the unmapped seas. I guess the idea was to scare off potential explorers from going to these literal uncharted waters.

I don’t really know how effective a strategy like that would be. I mean if there was a chance to actually see dragons, I’m pretty sure that’s quite a strong calling for a lot of folks out there.

Maybe that’s why it’s Here be Dragons and not Here be Paperwork.
